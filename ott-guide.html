OTT (Over-The-Top) platforms have become one of the most popular sources of entertainment today. With OTT subscriptions, users can watch movies, web series, sports and exclusive content online on multiple devices.

What is an OTT Subscription?

An OTT subscription is a digital service that allows users to stream content directly over the internet. These services work on mobile phones, smart TVs, laptops and tablets without the need for cable TV.
